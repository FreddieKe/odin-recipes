Lasagne Recipe

Description

A delicious and powerful Italian dish that will leave your guests asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chunk
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red or yellow, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 (28-ounce) can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  • chopped fresh oregano

Steps

  1. Chop ingredients
  2. Cook onions in pan
  3. Mix in tomatoes
  4. Put in oven for 30 mins
  5. Leave to cool and season to taste
