Stir fry recipe

Description

A delicious and powerful Vietnamese dish that will leave your guests asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • spring onions
  • chillis
  • baby sweetcorn
  • soy sauce
  • chicken
  • rice
  • peas

Steps

  1. Chop ingredients
  2. Cook chicken in oil on wok
  3. Cook vegetables
  4. Add soy sauce
  5. Leave to cool and season to taste
